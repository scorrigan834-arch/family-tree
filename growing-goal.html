Setting a savings goal

A goal turns "I wish" into "I'm on my way." The trick is breaking it into small, regular amounts.

Do the simple math

  • Write down the thing and its price.
  • Decide how much you can set aside each week.
  • Divide the price by that weekly amount — that's how many weeks until it's yours.

Seeing "eight weeks" instead of "someday" makes saving feel possible. Try our savings-goal and compound-interest tools to see it.
