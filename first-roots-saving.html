Saving for something you want

Saving is just waiting on purpose. Young kids feel "now" far more strongly than "later," so make the waiting visible and fun.

The clear-jar trick

  • Use a see-through jar so they watch the pile grow.
  • Tape a picture of the goal — a toy, a game — on the front.
  • Each time they add coins, count it together and see how close they are.

When they finally buy the goal with their own saved money, that pride is the whole point — the first taste of "I waited, and it was worth it."
