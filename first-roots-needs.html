Needs versus wants

Some things we need to live — food, a home, clothes, going to the doctor. Others are wants — toys, treats, the newest thing. Both are okay; the skill is telling them apart.

Make it a game

  • Name an item and have them call out "need!" or "want!"
  • Talk about the tricky ones — is a warm coat a need? Is this particular coat a want?

Kids who spot the difference early grow into adults who don't confuse the two at the store.
