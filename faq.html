FAQ

Is Family Tree free?

Family Tree is free to start with an account. We plan to offer optional paid plans later — for individuals who want more, and for organizations such as wealth-management firms.

Do I need an account?

You can look around to get a feel for it. Creating a free account lets Family Tree personalize your lessons and tools to your age and stage — and remember your progress. Some features, like your profile and the calculators, will ask you to sign in.

What do you do with my information?

We use details you share — like your age and life stage — to tailor what you see. We don't sell your personal information. See our Privacy page.

Is Family Tree for kids?

Yes — it's built for ages 5 to 50. For anyone under 18, a parent or guardian sets up and manages the account.

Is this financial advice?

No — it's general financial education. For decisions specific to your situation, talk with a qualified professional.
