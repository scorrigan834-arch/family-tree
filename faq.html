FAQ

Is Family Tree free?

Yes. Every lesson and tool is free to use.

Do you save my information?

No. There are no accounts, and the calculators run entirely in your browser — nothing you type is saved or sent anywhere.

Who is it for?

Anyone getting ready for a money decision — from a child's first dollar to an adult's biggest choices. See About.

Is this financial advice?

No — it's general financial education. For decisions specific to your situation, talk with a qualified professional.
