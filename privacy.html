What we collect

When you create an account, we collect what you give us — such as your email, your age or age range, your life stage, and where you are on your financial journey — so we can personalize your experience.

How we use it

To tailor lessons and tools to your stage, remember your progress, and understand how the site is used so we can improve it.

Children and families

Family Tree is built for ages 5 and up. Accounts for anyone under 18 are set up and managed by a parent or guardian, and we handle younger users' information in line with applicable children's-privacy laws.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

Your choices

You can ask to review or delete your information at any time by contacting us.

Calculators

The calculators run in your browser. Their results aren't stored unless you choose to save them to your profile.
