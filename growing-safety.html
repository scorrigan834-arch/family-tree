Money online: apps and scams

More and more of money lives on screens — apps, games, and websites. That's convenient, but it's also where people try to trick you out of money. Learning to spot the tricks keeps you and your money safe.

Common traps

  • In-app purchases that add up fast, especially inside games.
  • "You won a prize!" messages asking for info or payment — these are fake.
  • Fake shops or deals that look real but simply take your money.
  • Messages pretending to be a friend or company asking for money or passwords.

Stay safe

  • Never share passwords; use strong, different ones.
  • If a deal seems too good to be true, it is.
  • Check with a trusted adult before paying online or sharing personal details.
  • Real companies will never ask for your password.

Remember: slow down. Scams work by creating rush and excitement — pausing to think is your best protection.
