Estate planning and what to pass on

Estate planning isn't only for the wealthy — it's how you make sure what you've built goes where you intend, with less stress for the people you love.

The basics to have in place

  • A will that states who gets what, and who cares for any children.
  • Up-to-date beneficiaries on accounts and insurance (these override a will).
  • A plan for who can act for you if you can't.

And the piece most families miss: pass down the knowledge, not just the money. Heirs who understand how to handle wealth are far more likely to keep it — the whole idea behind Family Tree.
