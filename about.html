About Family Tree

Prepare the person, not just the portfolio.

Family Tree exists to give every child — and every adult still learning — the financial upbringing that sets them up for a lifetime of confident decisions.

Our mission

Financial confidence, one life stage at a time.

Most people are handed money responsibilities long before they’re taught how to handle them — a first paycheck, a first credit card, a first inheritance — and expected to figure it out. The result is stress, avoidable mistakes, and, in families with wealth, a generational gap that dissolves both fortunes and relationships.

Family Tree is built on a simple belief: money confidence isn’t one talk you have at twenty-five. It’s an upbringing — a series of small, age-appropriate lessons that start with a child’s first saved dollar and grow, decade by decade, into the judgment to steward a household, a portfolio, and a legacy.

Prepare the child for the money, not just the money for the child.

We serve two families of the same tree. For parents and their kids, we make the financial upbringing that prepared families work hard to give their heirs available to anyone — free to start. For advisors and the families they serve, we’re the education layer beneath the estate plan: the piece that prepares heirs to receive, steward, and grow what they inherit. What we believe

The principles behind everything here.

Education, never sales

We teach; we don’t sell. No products, no commissions, no hidden agenda — just clear, accurate financial education you can trust to share with your kids.

Grounded in real sources

Our lessons and guides are grounded in guidance from the CFPB, IRS, and SEC. It’s education, not personalized advice — and we’re always clear about the difference.

Age-appropriate, always

A five-year-old and a fifty-year-old don’t need the same thing. Every lesson is written for exactly where someone is in life — playful for kids, practical for adults.

Private, with no ads

Your family’s information stays yours. We don’t run ads and we don’t sell data. A calm, focused place to learn — nothing following you around.

Standards

Educational, not advice.

Family Tree provides general financial education to help you make informed decisions. It is not personalized financial, legal, or tax advice, and it isn’t a substitute for a professional who knows your specific situation. For decisions that matter, we encourage you to work with a qualified advisor, attorney, or tax professional — and we’re designed to make you a sharper, more confident partner in those conversations.

Our content is reviewed for accuracy and grounded in publicly available guidance from U.S. regulators and established financial principles. Where figures or statistics appear, we cite their sources.
